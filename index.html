The Philosophy

The Philosophy

Magic is not a trick. It is a way of seeing the world.

At Magos con Estilo, every artwork begins with a feeling. The moment when reality stops making sense. That instant of pure wonder — that is what we capture in every design.

This is not merchandise. This is wearable art. Each piece is crafted for those who understand that the true secret was never the method. It was the emotion.

We work with premium heavyweight cotton, digital hand-drawn illustrations, and a commitment to quality that honors the craft of magic itself.

— David Ortiz

About

I've been a magician since childhood. Growing up in Madrid with a deck of cards in my hands, watching the greats — Juan Tamariz, Arturo de Ascanio, Woody Aragón — I understood that magic isn't a trick: it's a way of seeing the world.

If I have to name the one who influenced me most, it's Dani DaOrtiz. He's a friend, a master, and I have immense affection for him. Also Gabi Pareras, and many others who don't fit here but whom I carry with me in every performance.

For me, magic is a way of life. It's not what I do, it's what I am. This collection reflects that: every artwork is a nod to those who understand that the true secret is not in the hand, but in the gaze.

— David Ortiz, Madrid
